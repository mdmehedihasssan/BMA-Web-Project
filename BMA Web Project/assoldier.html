Soldier

Recruitment in the Army is a common process to meet up combat requirement. Every male and female citizen, irrespective of caste,
class or religion is eligible for recruitment in the army provided he/she meets the laid down age, educational, physical and medical 
requirement. Recruitment is carried out on yearly basis (in two phases) by Headquarters Recruitment Unit (HQ RU)/Branch 
Recruitment Unit (BRU) and Training Centers.

Ineligiability

. Withdrawn/Discharged from Army/Navy/ Air Force or any Government service on any ground.

When to Apply

Recruitment schedule is announced through national dailies on half yearly basis. The vacancies are allotted as per district. Persons 
interested to join army will have to register through SMS of Teletalk mobile and then apply online to get the admit card along with 
examination date & venue. Following documents and materials to be carried to the recruiting vanue on examination day:
All educational certificates and mark sheets (Academic transcript). If photocopy then duly attested by First Class Gazatted Officer. 
Testimonial from school head with permanent address and date of birth. 
Consent certificate from parents duly attested by Chairman Union Council/Chairman Pourashava/Word Commissioner. 
Technical certificate from any government approved technical institution (for technical candidates). 
6 copies (5 cm x 4 cm) and 2 copies (2.5 cm x 2 cm) recent colour photograph duly attested by first class gazated officer. 
Necessary dress for swimming. 
Pen, pencil, scale and clipboard for written examination. 
Note : Children of serving/retired Army personnel may directly report to training centers on the day of the recruitment along with 
required documents. They will however only be recruited if they fulfill laid down standard.
